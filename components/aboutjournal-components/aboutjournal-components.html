About the Journal

The Journal of Advances in Management, Engineering, and Science (JAMES) is a scholarly open-access international journal that enables researchers to showcase their efforts through research papers across all fields of management, engineering, science, and their applications.

JAMES is dedicated to introducing advancements in new applications to researchers and academicians while providing foundational knowledge about research work.

The journal invites researchers to submit articles related to research findings, projects, surveys, and industrial experiences, with a focus on innovations and advancements in the areas of management, engineering, and science.

Frequency: JAMES issues Monthly Journals (Yearly 12 issues)

Aims & Scope

The Journal of Advances in Management, Engineering, and Science (JAMES) aims to be a leading platform for disseminating high-quality, peer-reviewed research in the fields of management, engineering, science, and their interdisciplinary applications. The journal fosters the exchange of innovative ideas, advances in theory, and practical solutions to real-world challenges.

Scope includes, but is not limited to:

Original research articles, case studies, and reviews in management, engineering, and science.

Studies presenting advancements in emerging technologies, methodologies, and applications.

Research on innovative management practices and their impact on organizations and industries.

Interdisciplinary studies that integrate management, engineering, and science for sustainable solutions.

Industrial projects, surveys, and experimental research contributing to academic knowledge and practical applications.

The journal is dedicated to bridging the gap between academic research and industry needs, encouraging collaboration between researchers, academicians, and practitioners worldwide.
