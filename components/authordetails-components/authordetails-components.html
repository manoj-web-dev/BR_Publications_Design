Biography

Dr. Jane Doe is a distinguished Professor of Educational Technology with over 20 years of experience in academia and research. She earned her Ph.D. in Educational Technology from Stanford University and has since been at the forefront of integrating digital innovations into educational practices.

Her research focuses on artificial intelligence in education, learning analytics, and digital transformation in higher education. Dr. Doe has published extensively in top-tier journals and has been invited as a keynote speaker at numerous international conferences.

She currently serves as the Director of the Center for Digital Learning Innovation and has received multiple awards for her contributions to educational technology research and practice.
