Author Guidelines

Articles will be evaluated in a blind peer review process. Academicians, research students, and scientists are always encouraged to submit their articles in our reputed journal. Research papers can be sent to the email address editor@james.com by stating that this manuscript is original and it has not been published in any journal and is not currently being considered for publication elsewhere. Editorial board has rights to take actions against the authors if the accepted articles may be published in the same form, either in the same language or translated form of the same content in any other language.

Publication Time

Original articles will be published in the online journal within 15 days. Authors are instructed to send publication fees with a copyright form as the sample mentioned here.

Publication Cost

Processing fee of Rs.1000 for Indian author and 25 USD for other nationals will be charged for publication. High quality research articles are exempted from processing fees if Editors-in-chief recommends for publication.

Style

Manuscripts should be typed in MS-Word and sent to the Editor via e-mail.

Length

A Transactions paper should not exceed 25 typeset, printed pages.

Structure of the Article

Title of article, authors' names, authors' institutional affiliations, abstract (200-300 words suitable for abstracting services), main text, tables (each starting with a caption) and figure captions, list of symbols and abbreviations (if necessary), numbered list of references, biographical sketch, and original figures.

We support open access dissemination to maximize visibility, citations, and impact within the global research community.

Author Names and Affiliations

As per the journal policy, co-authors who contribute to the article will be acknowledged or cited in the acknowledgements. Information related to the articles will be sent to the first author even though many authors contribute to the article.

Abstract

A short abstract (200-300 words maximum) is mandatory to introduce an article.

Purposes of Abstract


Clear Objectives

It offers a clear-cut objective, scope, and results of the paper and it also fascinates the readers.

Indexing & Retrieval

Key words mentioned in the abstract cause for indexing, abstracting, and retrieval purposes.

Content Overview

It does not focus on all the important points discussed in the article; it may not be helpful to readers for fast reading of the content.

Paper Acceptance

All the decisions related to publication will be taken by the Editor-in-Chief after a careful scrutiny of article done by Editorial Board Members.

Copyright

A declaration is to be attached with the article that states that it has been neither published nor submitted for publication, in whole or in part, either in a serial, professional journal or as a part in a book which is formally published and made available to the public. Authors should produce a consent letter to the publishers to publish and transfer of copyright form to protect the rights of the authors and publishers.

Proofs

Proofs will be sent to Co-authors via email and it should be corrected and to be returned within 7 days of receipt. The proofs should be used only for the corrections of typesetting errors; any other major corrections done by the publishers may be charged from the authors. Authors should assure that all the corrections are done and sent back to the publishers in one communication.
